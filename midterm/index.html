Welcome to Hogwarts!

About the Destination

Greetings! Hogwarts is a magical place and school that teaches young wizards and witches on their journey of wizardry. While these students are home for the summer, the school is open to tours and visits from the public!

Things to Do

Visitors will aboard a special train at Platform 9 ¾, followed by a boat ride to the school grounds. From there you can visit and explore the wonders of the historical building Hogwarts, the nearby property and lake, the nearby town of Hogsmeade, but make sure not to go get too close to the Forbidden Forest!

  • Examine The Creatures of the Campus
  • Tour Inside the Castle
  • Visit Hogsmeade

Book Your Stay!

Special guests stay in the student’s quarters to further immerse yourself in the experience. You will get sorted into 1 out of 4 houses: Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, or Hufflepuff. Make sure to learn the secret password to be able to get into your quarters, or the paintings won’t be able to let you in.
