From the Better User Experience Through Storytelling Articles: How can framing your project in a story format help you refine your ideas? Write a paragraph explanation.

Framing your project in a story format can help you refine your ideas because of the way it helps your audience guide through your project, engage in it, and remember it in the best way possible. By using storytelling, it enhances your project and gives it humanity, a better overall experience, and a better understanding. Like Dorelle Rabinowitz states,” You can get designers, engineers, product managers, strategists and execs jazzed about a proposed feature because of a story, and it can be extremely fulfilling.” It is important to get people excited about your project, or they’ll forget it. Christian Saylor said, “If you’re telling the right story to the wrong audience, or even telling the right story the wrong way, then your business or product will ultimately fade away.” As you can see, it is important to frame your project in a story format so that it’s better for your audience and your staple in their lives.

I find myself needing a certain aid that I can’t find anywhere on the internet without searching endlessly. With this project, I want to expand on the accessibilty of information when it comes to designing and organizing it for my audience to find what they need easily.
Audience My audience are females or males in their mid-teens to mid-twenties that are majority of beginner designers, experienced designers who are older and in need inspiration for new projects or interested in learning current trends, or people who are just looking to learn new skills for fun.
Competition The biggest competition would probably be professional teaching websites that have professional teachers with courses that are often required to be paid for. They offer organized teachings, but people shouldn’t pay for something they might already know or something so simple that it could have been free.
Key Messages Designers often don’t know where to start on their projects, tasks, or assignments. With a website such as this one, it would be easier for people to reach the kind of information they want to obtain. The whole thing is also completely free, and it highlights current designers in the field who want to share their skills.
Call to Action When the audience sees this project, they would be ecstatic to find such an easy navigating website to find all the things that they want to do try-out or find themselves eager share their own findng.
