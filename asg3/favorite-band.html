My Favorite Band: Stray Kids

Stray Kids is an 8 member Korean Pop Band that has been around since 2018. They got their name from the idea of bringing together lost souls, guiding them, and going through life together. They have a name for their fans, called "Stay,"connecting to their orgins and meaning of their band name. They Are currently under the Korean company JYP Entertainment, and they are widely known for their self-produced music. One of their most recent hits is "Ceremony," the main track on their full album named "Karma."

Why They Are My Favorite

The reason why they are my favorite is because of the feeling of comfort they often bring to their fans. They make me feel like I belong somewhere and that I will always find comfort in their music. Their music is also just really good, and they have such a large variety of music that can easily match any of my moods. Some of their music is really hype, some others are playful and fun, while others can be sad and somber. I agree that their sound isn't for everyone, but their messages and music reside in me very deeply.
