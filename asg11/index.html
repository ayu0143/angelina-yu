My Dodgeball Team: The Saw Sharks

The Saw Sharks are from Keller, Texas. They are always ready to cut their way to victory with their razor sharp personalities and skill. With their 5 players, they are ruthless sharks in the water ready to hunt their prey. You can find them practicing in their home gym, or maybe at the aquarium where majority of them like to go in their free time. They're mean, ruthless, and ready to always win.
